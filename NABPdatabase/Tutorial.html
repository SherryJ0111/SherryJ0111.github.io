Tutorial

1.Select your interesting organism,interaction type or classification

NABP database provides two ways of searching this database: one is by selecting organism of the corresponding protein ,the interaction type(protein-DNA and protein_RNA interaction) or the classification of protein chains.
The other is searching the keywords globally, the result will list with matched data.

2.Detail imformation about interacting protein and nucleic acid

For each result returned by NABP database,users can get detail sequence information and binding site annotations by simply clicking the plus symbol at the left side of the table

3.Download

To facilite the relevent research, all the data on the NABP database are publicly available for non-commercial use, distribution, and reproduction in any medium ,you can download them from the download page.
